BlockChain Implementation

Group 11 Amazon GO Store Sprint 1

This is the demo for Sprint 1, demonstrating the proof of concept for implementation of a blockchain. The blockchain will serve as a record of purchase, with 1 block per purchase per individual customer. One chain, the master chain, will be kept on the company server. Other chains will be distributed to each customer. This distribution ensures accuracy of transactions.
